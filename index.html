The Problem

Most crypto wallets prioritize speed‚Äîexecuting transactions instantly once signed. But in today‚Äôs environment, attackers often gain remote access to devices rather than stealing seed phrases. Once inside, they can trigger transactions and drain funds in seconds, often before users even realize their wallet is compromised. Speed, in this case, becomes a vulnerability‚Äînot a feature.

Solution: 30-Day Time Lock

Slowlyy introduces a fixed 30-day delay on all transactions. This time buffer gives users the opportunity to:

  • Detect unauthorized activity
  • Revoke access
  • Move funds to a freshly secured wallet if their current device is compromised

All transaction requests are stored but delayed, only executable after 30 full days have passed.

Benefits

  • Available on desktop and mobile for secure access anytime, anywhere
  • Drastically reduces risk of fast-draining malware
  • No third-party custody: users still own their private keys
  • Tamper-resistant validation via dual sources and cryptographic signatures
  • User-friendly recovery buffer: aligns with real-world financial risk controls
